Bio

Adversor were founded back in September 2015 by Dado. He quickly called his brother Ja and begun to play, short after they met Aurora and the band came to life when Ema completed the line-up on bass duty. The young band released their "Rise To Survive" debut album in October 2016, which was greatly welcomed by fans and critics. In 2017 they toured Europe to promoite RTS.

After the tour Aurora parted ways with Adversor and was replaced by Phil at lead guitar. In few months they got back to the studios where they recorded the second album "The End Of Mankind", a good success from critics and fans. They embarked in a long tour playing the UK for the first time.

In November '18, after their second European Tour supporting "The End Of Mankind", Ema had to quit the band and was replaced by Ale. The band toured non stop in 2018 and 2019 gaining more fans playing shows supporting acts such as Exciter, Protector, Nifelheim, Gama Bomb and many more. Currently they're in the studio recording their 3rd full-lenght.
